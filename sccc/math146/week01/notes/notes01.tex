  \section{Time Graphs}

  \subsection{Overview}

  Time graphs show how a continuous variable changes over time.  

  \begin{itemize*}
    \item x-axis is time
    \item y-axis is the quantity being measured
    \item can use line or bar
  \end{itemize*}
