  \item p. 430, question 43

\begin{solution}
The length of the diagonal is $d = \sqrt{2 a^2} = a \sqrt{2}$.  

So 
\[
  \tan \theta = \frac{\cancel{a}}{\cancel{a} \sqrt{2}} = \frac{\sqrt{2}}{2}
\]

And:
\[
  \theta = \arctan \frac{\sqrt{2}}{2} = 35.3 \degree
\]

\end{solution}
